About us

We can be called marketers, brokers and traders at the same time. We market the products of our clients, broker the deal and at times take positions in a trade. For the wider good of the trading businesses, we optimize technology and systems to connect multiple dots of the entire trade value chain.This will not only enhance productivity but help in improving liquidity management, cost reduction and risk management.

Our experts collect, analyze and publish real time data on physical commodities in the market. The prices are the signals coming from a vast network of traders and other stakeholders in the trading business.

Trades are executed on our enterprise grade proprietary systems to ensure that buyers and suppliers have fair fields to play. We ensure transparency in our processes and executions to build long term trust. Post execution, we provide logistics/supply chain management across multiple geographies and reporting of the entire transaction end to end to the parties involved.

Value addition
